About Consultant Communicator
Consultant Communicator (ConsultComm) is a program that allows anyone managing multiple projects, clients or tasks to effectively keep track of exactly how long they've spent on each project.

Let's say you're a consultant. And let's say you have about five clients. And let's say you work on three or four tasks each (answering calls, writing code, going to meetings, working on your goofy open-source Java project, etc.) That's twenty tasks you have to keep track of, and of course since you bill by the hour clients want detailed invoices. They just won't leave you alone. And they keep calling, and calling...

ConsultComm to the rescue! You can easily keep track of every second spent on projects and spent away doing other stuff. Not only that, you can export the time kept by ConsultComm to a database so you can automate your invoices and accounting. Plus times and preferences are stored in XML, so you can manipulate the numbers to your twisted heart's content.
